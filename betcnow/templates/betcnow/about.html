What is Betcnow?

Betcnow is a low bitcoin betting website under a lottery system called Betcpot where you can multiply up to 255 times your play, get passive income through a referral system and participate for a free Bitcoin every month.

How does it works?

Each Betcpot has 1000 numbers for sale that will be available for three days or until sold out, the minimum quantity of numbers sold to close a Betcpot is 135. Winners will be announced three hours after the closing of each Betcpot and will be awarded More than 13% of the total numbers sold. On the other hand, Betcnow has a referral system that allows you to win up to 10% of each of the plays made by your referrals. Our users with membership participate in a points system called Bitcoin Race where the best member of each month will be awarded with 1 Bitcoin for free.

STEP 1. Join Betcnow

It's very easy and fast. Enter a username, your email address and an alphanumeric password of at least 8 characters. If you are referred by another user you can click on the word "Edit" to enter his username and be part of their referred, otherwise, betcnow will be your sponsor. After confirming your password, enter in the email address you specified and confirm your account by clicking on the link you receive. That is all!.

STEP 2. Pick your favorite number

After clicking the play button, you can select a maximum of 8 numbers between 000 and 999 in the Betcpot, or you can type how many numbers you want to choose and click the random button to select them at random. The green color in the numbers means that it is available, the red color means that it was already bought and the blue color means that it was already purchased and it was a number with a gift prize. After selecting your numbers, click on the blue play button and proceed to pay. If you want to play more than 8 numbers in the Betcpot, you must repeat the process.

STEP 3. Pay

On the check-out page you will be shown the Betcpot number you are playing in, the quantity of numbers played and the total to be paid plus the payment processor's commissions, once you have confirmed this information, you must copy and paste your Wallet address by clicking the "Copy Address" button, or you can simply scan the QR code provided by your wallet address provider from your mobile device, and that's it!

STEP 4. Wait for results

A few hours after closing each Betcpot you can see the winning numbers on the "Results" page, in the "This week results" section you can see the result of each Betcpot closed in the week by clicking on it. You can also see how Bitcoin's Race is going for the date in the "Bitcoin-Race Points" section.

Once inside the Betcpot results, the first three winners are shown on our podium where you can see their username, their winning number and the amount won in bitcoin. Below the same page the winners corresponding to the groups Gold, Silver, Bronze and Copper are shown indicating the names of users, the amount won in bitcoin for each group and for each user and the winning number.
