What is Betcnow?

Betcnow is a low-cost bitcoin gambling website which consists in a lottery system called Betcpot where you can multiply up to 255 times your bet, get passive income through a referral system and participate for a big prize in BTC every month.

How does it works?

Each Betcpot has 1000 numbers for sale. At 20:00 UTC of every day, the betcpots get closed regardless of how many numbers has been sold. All draws are made considering only the numbers sold, and more than 13% of them will be awarded. Winners will be announced approximately three hours after the closing of each Betcpot.

On the other hand, Betcnow has a referral system that allows you to win the 5 or 10 percent of each of the bets made by your referrals, depending on the membership you have.

Every user will be participating for a monthly prize in BTC, based in a points system, called Bitcoin Race.

STEP 1 Join Betcnow

It's very easy and fast. Enter a username, your email address and an alphanumeric password of at least 8 characters. If you are referred by another user you can click on the word "Edit" to enter his username, otherwise, betcnow will be your sponsor. After confirming your password, check your email and confirm your account by clicking on the link you receive. That's all!.

STEP 2 Pick your favorite numbers

For every Betcpot you can select a maximum of 8 numbers between 000 and 999, or you can type how many numbers you want to choose and click the random button to select them at random. The green color in the numbers means that it is available, the red color means that it was already bought and the blue color means that it was already purchased and it was a number with an instant gift prize. After selecting your numbers, click on button and proceed to pay. If you want to select more than 8 numbers in the Betcpot, you must repeat the process.

STEP 3 Pay

On the check-out page you will see the Betcpot number you are playing in, the quantity of numbers played and the total bitcoins to be paid. Once you have confirmed this information, you must send the bitcoins to the address displayed in the payment box. You can copy and paste this address or you can simply scan the QR code with your mobile device, if you have any bicoin wallet app intalled.
